air distribution flap motor : removal - refitting

RPR-000468084

AVERTISSEMENT:
Before carrying out any operation on a vehicle fitted with the Stop&Start system, always follow the safety instructions(voir, vehicle : precautions for the repair).

removal

Removal preparation operation

Disconnect the battery(voir, 12 V battery : removal - refitting).
Remove the centre console passenger’s foot deflector(voir, centre console assembly : exploded view).

Removal operation

Disconnect:
  • the tie-rod1 from the distribution motor,
  • the distribution motor connector2.
Remove(voir, passenger compartment heating and ventilation assembly : exploded view):
  • the distribution motor bolt,
  • the distribution motor.

refitting

Proceed in the reverse order to removal.
